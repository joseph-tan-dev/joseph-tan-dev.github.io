"Honorable Mention"

My friend worked for a marketing agency that went into a contract with Disney to promote the upcoming animated film "Finding Dory". They finished the design for the meme generator but was in lack of a developer who could make that happen.

The deadline was looming, I helped him by building the generator from ground up within 2 days - during the weekend.

ReactJS, jQuery, FileSaver, GifJS, HTML2Canvas, and FabricJS were used. It is by far the most interesting project I've built, and also in the shortest amount of time.

Hey, Dory fans, feel free to download the wallpaper and the meme.
